About Deakon

Built on a belief that home improvement should be done differently — with genuine service, skilled craftsmanship, and respect for your home.

Our Story

Why We Built Deakon Differently

Chris Sockwell founded Deakon Home Services in 2011 after years of seeing a troubling pattern across the home improvement industry: poor service quality treated as acceptable, customers disrespected, and craftsmanship cut short for margin.

Chris set out to prove that you could run a profitable, growing company while genuinely putting service first — not as a marketing line, but as an operating principle.

"When you call us, you'll speak with real people. When we show up, we show up on time. When we finish, we leave your home cleaner than we found it."

Today, the Deakon team brings that same conviction to every job in the Greater Atlanta area — from a single window replacement to a full-home renovation.
